**Scout Vespers**  
**Standard Campfire Closing**  
**Tune: “Oh Christmas Tree” or “O’ Tannenbaum”**   
*This song has been there throughout my scouting years, closing campfires in a way few other songs could. There’s nothing like an entire camp staff singing this song as the fire fades before scouts slowly file out. In my opinion, you can’t ever end a campfire without this song.*

Softly falls the light of day  
As our campfire fades away  
Silently each scout should ask  
Have I done my daily task?  
Have I kept my honor bright?  
Have I guiltless sleep tonight?  
Have I done and have I dared  
Everything to be prepared?

*Second, less used verse*  
Listen Lord, oh listen Lord  
As I whisper soft and low  
Bless my Mom and bless my Dad  
There is something they should know  
I have kept my honor bright  
The oath and law have been my guide  
Mom and Dad this you should know  
Deep in my heart I love you so
