**Quartermaster’s Store**  
**Long Time Standard Boy Scout Song**  
**Tune: Probably original**  
*Talk to any long time scouter and they’ll surely know this song. Probably dates back to the early days of Boy Scouts, if not the military before that. Whatever the case, you know it now.*
