**Classic Song**  
**Tune: There’ll Be A Hot Time In The Old Town Tonight**  
*This song is apparently about the great Chicago fire that, legend has it, was started by a cow kicking over a lantern. The fire burned up most of Chicago. Fire safety isn’t just relevant in the forest\! It’s actually a pretty old song which is more commonly known as Old Mother Leary, but this is a Lawton song book, so here we have it as it is sung at Lawton. Ah the power of writing your own book\!*

One dark night  
When we were all in bed  
Old Lady Leary left a lantern in the shed  
And when the cow kicked it over  
She winked her eye and said,  
“There’ll be a hot time  
In the old town Tonight\!”
