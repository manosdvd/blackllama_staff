**Hey Ho, Nobody Home**  
Traditional Round  
Tune: Classic, similar to Rose Red  
*I actually heard the root of this song once, as part of a much larger folk gospel song. This part however is simply the part everyone knows and makes for a great round. Also good with America, America, and Rose Red.* 

Hey ho, nobody home  
Meat nor sleep nor money have I none  
But I will still be merry…  
*Continue round in 3 parts*
