**One Fat Hen**  
**Repeat After Me Thing**  
**Tune: Spoken**  
*The history of "One Fat Hen" is particularly fascinating, as it traces its origins not to traditional acoustic music, but rather to its use as a mid-twentieth-century announcer's test for radio broadcasters. The sequence was explicitly designed by vocal coaches to test breath control, precise enunciation, and short-term memory recall under pressure.*
*Every camp does this a little different. This is Lawton’s variation. It’s fun to get good at it and then watch the audience squirm as you wait for them to repeat you.*

*Start with the first line, repeat adding the next line each time till at the end you’re doing all 10 in one run.*
