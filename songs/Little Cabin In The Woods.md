**Little Cabin In The Woods**  
**Common Camp Action Song**  
**Tune: Unknown**  
*This one is very popular at many camps. Possibly an old song just now making its comeback, or relatively new and still spreading. Either way, it’s a good one. Also see Crazy Herman, the “rock” version.*  
*Actions are self explanatory.*

Little cabin in the woods  
Little man by the window stood  
Saw a rabbit hopping by  
Rapping at his door.

“Help me\! Help me\! Help me\!” He cried,  
“Or the hunter’ll shoot me dead”  
“Come little rabbit, come inside.  
Safely to abide.”
