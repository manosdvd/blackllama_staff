**Staffer Song**  
**Almost Mandatory Tradition**  
**Tune: Original**  
*This is a tradition at Lawton and many other camps as well. It’s the highlight of the end of the week campfire at Lawton, the only skit/song the staff does on Friday night, and the funniest they do all week.*

*Chorus*  
If I were a staffer a staffer I would be  
But if I weren’t a staffer  
\_\_\_\_\_\_\_ I would be….

*Verses are done by each person left to right. The first person will do his/her verse twice, then step back. The second person (and progressively right) will do his/her verse twice, then everyone to the left of that person will step up and all will do their verse twice more with everyone to the right stands and waits. For instance, the last person will step up, do his/her verse, then everyone will step up and do their verse, preferably louder than before. Any time you can place parts that can interact with each other (like the actor/Lassie and the statue), it can be very funny. There is a very long list of verses here, pick and choose at most 8-10 of these to do. DO NOT have the entire staff on stage. The song should not be 20 minutes long.*

A Plumber  
Flush it, plunge it look out below *(with plunger)*

A Carpenter  
Six by Four\! Nail it to the floor\!  *(with measuring tape)*

Lady MacBeth  
OUT, OUT, OUT DAMN SPOT\!\!   *(scrubbing hands)*

A Birdwatcher  
There’s an eagle\! There’s a hawk\! There’s a pidgeon\! SPLAT\!

A Farmer  
Give Bessy\! Give\! The kids have got to live\!

A Baby  
Mommy\! Daddy\! I wuv yoo\!

A Washer Woman  
Washy washy, washy washy, hang those clothes dry\!

A Fireman  
Jump lady\! Jump\! Ahhhhhh SPLAT\!\!

An Exterminator  
Squish Em\! Squash Em\! Nuke em till they glow\!
