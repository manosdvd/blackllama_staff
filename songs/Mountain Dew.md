**Mountain Dew**  
**Funny Soda Song**  
**Tune: Probably Original**  
*Not much to say about this song except that the original use of the term “Mountain Dew” would be inappropriate for scouts, so thank goodness the caffeinated beverage we know today was created. Now it’s just a reference to the strength of the yellowy substance.* 

*Chorus*  
Oh they call it that good old Mountain Dew-Dew-Dew  
And them that refuse it are few (darn few\!)  
I’ll shut my mug if you’ll fill up my jug  
With that good old Mountain Dew

My cousin Jake he got bit by a snake  
We thought that he wouldn’t pull through  
But he danced a jig when we gave him a swig  
Of that good ol’ Mountain Dew

My Auntie Bess she has hair on her chest  
We asked her if she put it there with glue (or paste)  
She said, “Heck no\! It just started to grow  
When I took up that good ol’ Mountain Dew

My Auntie June got some new perfume  
And it had a most peculiar phew  
Well imagine her surprise when we had it analyzed  
It was nothing but that good ol’ Mountain Dew

My brother Nort he is sawed-off and short  
He stood about four foot two (or three)  
But he acts like a giant when you give him a pint  
Of that good ol’ Mountain Dew

Old Uncle Ned out back had a shed  
With a seat that’s about two by two (or three)  
Well you can’t go there no more cause it’s piled from top to floor  
With cases of that good ol’ Mountain Dew
