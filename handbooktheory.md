Staff handbook site

User progression 

1. Scout visits the app  
2. App promotes Camp Lawton   
3. Scout clicks on “apply”  
4. Scout asked to create user (will always create as Candidate and admin will change it later)  
5. Scout completes application and submits  
6. Camp Director reviews application and rejects (with a short note on why), accepts, or “pending” (with additional information that needs to be gathered)   
7. If accepted, an email inviting them to complete onboarding. If rejected, they'll get a courteous email saying they weren't chosen with a short note on why, or pending with further instructions.   
8. Accepted automatically shifts them to Staff role.   
9. Staff have access to a profile system (visible only to staff and admins) that will automatically populate with some info (Minors under 18 will have limited info, and otherwise staff can opt out, then it will only show first name and position \- position assigned by camp director on application approval). Staff can also fill in data in the profile to get to know each other.   
10. A forum system (reddit-like) will be available to chat, moderated by admins and prominently featuring scouting values and Safeguarding Youth guidelines which apply to digital interactions as well.   
11. As much of the onboarding can be done directly on this site as possible, and links for downloads of the rest. 

Admins have the ability to edit content on the site via an intuitive wysiwyg gui. 

Dashboard  
For guests, focus on telling about camp Lawton history and culture. Visitors still have access to training and staff expectations and chain of command, no need to gatekeep, but policies and procedures are registered users only. Onboarding is staff only and becomes the primary highlighted feature until the checklist is complete. That checklist should have a confirmation as received marked by the admin. Probably should be added to the supabase database.   
Once onboarding is complete, training and policies and procedures takes priority. Have a manually maintained “Updates” box for a blog of sorts about current news and upcoming trainings and events. Each post there should be set to user level (check boxes for who can see it, everyone, candidate, staff, admin). There should be a wysiwyg gui for that too. 

The app should be installable and accessible offline (though updated regularly when online for the blog and other feeds)
